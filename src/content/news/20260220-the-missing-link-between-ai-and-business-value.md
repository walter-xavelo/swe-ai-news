## Overview

On February 20, 2026, JetBrains published "The Missing Link Between AI and Business Value". Meaningful AI performance measurement is not about bigger numbers or more dashboards. It is about connecting operational signals with business truth.

The item fits the month’s broader shift toward AI-assisted software delivery, where product value increasingly comes from usable workflows, safer defaults, and clearer operational controls.

## Why This Matters

This matters because AI tooling is now being judged not just by demo quality but by measurable adoption, throughput, and organization-wide ROI.

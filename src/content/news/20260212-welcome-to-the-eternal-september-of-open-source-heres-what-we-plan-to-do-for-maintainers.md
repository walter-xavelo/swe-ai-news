## Overview

On February 12, 2026, GitHub published "Welcome to the Eternal September of open source. Here’s what we plan to do for maintainers.". Open source is hitting an “Eternal September.” As contribution friction drops, maintainers are adapting with new trust signals, triage approaches, and community-led solutions.

The item fits the month’s broader shift toward AI-assisted software delivery, where product value increasingly comes from usable workflows, safer defaults, and clearer operational controls.

## Why This Matters

This matters because developer tooling competition is increasingly about workflow leverage and operational fit, not just raw model novelty.

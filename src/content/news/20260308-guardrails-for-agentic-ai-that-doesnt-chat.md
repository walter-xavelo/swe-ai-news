Forbes argued that the real governance problem in agentic AI is not conversational UX but the hidden, automated execution layer, where permissions, observability, and guardrails matter most.

For engineering teams, the practical takeaway is that security controls now have to account for AI-assisted workflows, automated access paths, and a faster cadence of policy and dependency change.

## Overview

GitHub updated Copilot usage metrics so organizations can identify active coding agent users. The change gives enterprise teams better visibility into who is actually using more autonomous workflows instead of just having Copilot seats assigned.

## Why It Matters For SWE

AI tool adoption is moving into a measurement phase. Leaders want to know whether agentic features are actually changing engineering behavior, where usage is concentrated, and whether those workflows deserve broader rollout.

## Key Signals

- Enterprise AI adoption is being managed with finer-grained telemetry.
- Seat counts are no longer enough to evaluate tool impact.
- Agentic workflows are becoming a distinct category of usage.
- Metrics are shaping the next wave of AI tooling decisions.

## SWE Industry Takeaway

The market is shifting from “who bought it?” to “who is using it, how, and with what outcome?” Better telemetry is becoming part of the AI product story.

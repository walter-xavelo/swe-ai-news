## Overview

On February 22, 2026, TechCrunch published "All the important news from the ongoing India AI Impact Summit". India is hosting a four-day AI Summit this week that will be attended by executives from major AI labs and Big Tech, including OpenAI, Anthropic, Nvidia, Microsoft, Google, and Cloudflare, as well as heads of state.

The item fits the month’s broader shift toward AI-assisted software delivery, where product value increasingly comes from usable workflows, safer defaults, and clearer operational controls.

## Why This Matters

This matters because the bottleneck for AI products is increasingly operational: infrastructure, deployment, and cost control now shape what teams can ship.
